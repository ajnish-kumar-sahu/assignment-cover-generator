📝 How to Use

Everything you need to create professional assignment covers

Welcome Guide

  • On your first visit, a welcome modal gives you quick tips
  • Click "How to Use" to open this detailed guide anytime
  • Check "Don't show again" to skip the welcome on future visits
  • The help button (?) in the top-left corner always opens this guide

Getting Started

  • Fill in your details in the form on the left side
  • Required fields:
    • Student Name, University Roll No., Class Roll No.
    • Semester, Subject, Course Code
    • Submission Date, Assignment Type
  • Choose from 7 professional templates
  • Select a theme color to match your institution
💡 Pro Tip: Your data auto-saves every 30 seconds — you won't lose your work!

Managing Your Data

  • Auto-save: Work saves automatically every 30 seconds
  • Manual save: Click "Save" or press Ctrl+S
  • Load: Restore saved details with "Load" button or Ctrl+L
  • Reset: Clear all fields using the ↺ floating button
  • Data is stored securely in your browser's local storage

Export Options

  • PDF — Best for digital submissions, perfect formatting
  • JPG — Great for sharing on platforms, universal compatibility
  • Print — Direct printing with proper margins
🖨️ Print Tip: Use A4 paper and enable "Background Graphics" in print settings for best quality.

Theme & Accessibility

  • 3-mode theme: Auto (follows system & time), Light, Dark
  • Auto mode activates dark theme after 7 PM and light theme at 6 AM
  • Adjustable font sizes for better readability
  • Full keyboard navigation support
  • Interactive particle background responds to mouse movement

Troubleshooting

  • Preview not updating? — Check required fields are filled, try switching templates
  • Preview blank? — Update browser, disable ad-blockers, check internet
  • PDF not downloading? — Allow pop-ups, try Chrome, check storage space
  • Data not loading? — Enable local storage, don't use incognito mode
  • Display issues on mobile? — Use portrait orientation, zoom out if needed
